<p><p>The Modular Cleanroom System market is primarily categorized into Mono-Block Systems and Modular Wall Systems. Mono-Block Systems offer a single, pre-fabricated unit that integrates all cleanroom components for rapid deployment, ensuring efficiency and lower labor costs. In contrast, Modular Wall Systems involve assembling individual wall panels, allowing for customizable layouts and easier modifications, making them ideal for evolving production needs. Both types are designed to maintain controlled environments essential for industries like pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and electronics.</p></p>
