<p><p>The Smart Stacker market is categorized into two primary types: trackless stackers and tracked stackers. Trackless stackers operate independently, utilizing wheels for mobility on smooth surfaces, making them ideal for warehouses and logistics. In contrast, tracked stackers feature a tracked system for enhanced stability and maneuverability on uneven terrains, suitable for construction or outdoor settings. Both types enhance warehouse efficiency but cater to different operational needs, offering flexibility in material handling and storage solutions across various industries.</p></p>
