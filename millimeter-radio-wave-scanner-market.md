<p><strong>The Millimeter Radio-Wave Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Scanner</li><li>Active Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The millimeter radio-wave scanner market includes two main types: passive scanners and active scanners. Passive scanners detect naturally emitted radio waves from objects without emitting any signals themselves, making them suitable for stealthy surveillance applications. In contrast, active scanners emit radio waves and analyze the reflected signals to produce detailed images of objects or structures, allowing for enhanced scanning capabilities. Both types serve critical roles in security, safety, and industrial applications, adapting to specific operational needs and environments.</p></p>
