<p><p>The breast milk pasteurizer market is divided into two main types: water-free pasteurizers and with-water pasteurizers. Water-free pasteurizers use advanced technologies like steam or infrared heating to eliminate bacteria without the need for water, making them more efficient and compact. In contrast, with-water pasteurizers rely on heated water to pasteurize the milk, ensuring that temperatures are maintained uniformly. Both types aim to ensure the safety and nutritional integrity of breast milk for infants, catering to varying consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
