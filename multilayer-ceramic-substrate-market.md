<p><p>The multilayer ceramic substrate market is primarily divided into two types: Aluminum Nitride (AlN) multilayered ceramic substrates and Alumina multilayered ceramic substrates. Aluminum Nitride substrates are favored for their superior thermal conductivity and electrical insulation properties, making them ideal for high-power electronics. In contrast, Alumina substrates are more widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and good mechanical strength, suitable for various electronic applications. Both types cater to the increasing demand for efficient thermal management in electronics.</p></p>
