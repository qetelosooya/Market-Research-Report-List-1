<p><strong>The Connected Vehicle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded System</li><li>Tethered System</li><li>Integrated System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The connected vehicle market consists of three main types: Embedded Systems, Tethered Systems, and Integrated Systems. Embedded Systems are fully integrated technologies within the vehicle, providing real-time data processing and connectivity without reliance on external devices. Tethered Systems connect to a smartphone or external device, enhancing functionality while relying on the mobile platform for connectivity. Integrated Systems combine both embedded components and cellular networks, offering seamless connectivity and advanced features, allowing vehicles to interact with infrastructure and other devices effectively.</p></p>
