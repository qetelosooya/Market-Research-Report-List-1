<p><p>Silicon nitride crucibles are essential in the semiconductor market due to their high thermal stability and chemical resistance. Hot-pressed silicon nitride crucibles are manufactured under high pressure and temperature for superior strength. Reaction-bonded silicon nitride crucibles are formed through a chemical reaction, offering good thermal shock resistance. Sintered silicon nitride crucibles are created by compacting and heating silicon nitride powder, resulting in dense, durable products. Isostatically pressed silicon nitride crucibles achieve uniform density and performance by using equal pressure in all directions during production.</p></p>
