<p><ul><li>Isostatic Pressure Graphite</li><li>Die-Pressed Graphite</li><li>Extruded Graphite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic graphite materials market is categorized into three main types: isostatic pressure graphite, die-pressed graphite, and extruded graphite. Isostatic pressure graphite is produced under uniform pressure, leading to superior density and uniformity, making it ideal for high-performance applications. Die-pressed graphite is formed using a die and is suited for various industrial uses, offering good mechanical properties. Extruded graphite is created by forcing graphite paste through a die, allowing for continuous shapes and is commonly used in electrical applications and seals.</p></p>
