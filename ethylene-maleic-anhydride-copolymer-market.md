<p><p>Ethylene Maleic Anhydride Copolymer (EMA) is a thermoplastic copolymer derived from the polymerization of ethylene and maleic anhydride. This material combines the flexibility of ethylene with the reactive and crosslinkable properties of maleic anhydride, making it suitable for various applications, including coatings, adhesives, and packaging. Its unique characteristics, such as good adhesion, chemical resistance, and thermal stability, contribute to its growing popularity in industries like automotive, electronics, and construction.</p><p>The market for Ethylene Maleic Anhydride Copolymer is witnessing significant growth, fueled by rising demand in packaging applications and advancements in material technologies.
