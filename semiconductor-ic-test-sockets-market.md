<p><p>The semiconductor IC test sockets market is categorized into several types, including BGA (Ball Grid Array), QFN (Quad Flat No-lead), WLCSP (Wafer-Level Chip Scale Package), and others. BGA sockets facilitate testing of high-density packages with balls arranged in a grid, while QFN sockets accommodate flat, leadless designs for improved thermal performance. WLCSP sockets enable testing at the wafer level, enhancing efficiency. Each socket type serves unique applications, catering to advancements in electronic packaging technologies.</p></p>
