<p><p>The water meter measuring capsule market includes two primary types: multi-jet and single jet water meter measuring capsules. Multi-jet capsules use multiple jets to measure water flow, providing high accuracy and efficiency, making them suitable for residential and commercial applications. In contrast, single jet capsules operate on a simpler mechanism with a single flow jet, ideal for lower flow rates and typically used in smaller applications. Both types are essential for accurate water consumption measurement and efficient resource management.</p></p>
