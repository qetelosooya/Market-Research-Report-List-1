<p><ul><li>Instrument-based Inspection</li><li>Platform-based Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cancer screening technology encompasses various methodologies, primarily categorized into instrument-based and platform-based inspections. Instrument-based inspection involves specialized devices that detect cancer markers or abnormalities through imaging techniques, biopsies, or blood tests, emphasizing precision and targeted analysis. In contrast, platform-based inspection utilizes integrated systems or software platforms that streamline the screening process, often combining multiple diagnostic tools for comprehensive assessments. Both types play crucial roles in early detection, helping to improve patient outcomes and optimize cancer management strategies.</p></p>
