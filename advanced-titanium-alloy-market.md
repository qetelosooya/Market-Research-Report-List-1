<p><p>The advanced titanium alloy market is categorized into three main types: α (alpha) titanium alloys, β (beta) titanium alloys, and α+β titanium alloys. α titanium alloys are characterized by their excellent corrosion resistance and high-temperature stability, making them suitable for aerospace applications. β titanium alloys offer enhanced strength and ductility, ideal for manufacturing complex parts. α+β titanium alloys combine the properties of both types, providing a versatile solution for various high-performance applications in industries like aerospace and biomedical.</p></p>
