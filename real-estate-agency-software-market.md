<p><p>Real estate agency software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, providing easy access from any internet-enabled device, promoting collaboration and reducing IT costs. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on an agency's own servers, offering greater control and data security but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Choosing between these options depends on the agency's needs, budget, and technology infrastructure preferences.</p></p>
