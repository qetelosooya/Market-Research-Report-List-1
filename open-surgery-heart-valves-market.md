<p><p>The open surgery heart valves market is primarily categorized into mechanical valves and Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement (TAVR) valves. Mechanical valves are durable devices designed for long-term use, typically made from materials like titanium or carbon, and require lifelong anticoagulation therapy. TAVR valves are minimally invasive and suitable for patients deemed high-risk for traditional surgery. They are deployed via catheter through the vascular system, offering quicker recovery times and reduced hospital stays, reshaping treatment options for aortic stenosis.</p></p>
