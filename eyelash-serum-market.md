<p><p>The eyelash serum market includes several types: peptide-based serums promote eyelash growth by nourishing hair follicles with amino acids. Vitamin-based serums enhance lash health and appearance through vitamins such as biotin and panthenol. Prostaglandin-derived serums stimulate eyelash growth by mimicking natural prostaglandins, often leading to longer and thicker lashes. Eyelash primers, while not growth enhancers, prepare lashes for mascara application, improving volume and length, and providing a base for better makeup adherence.</p></p>
