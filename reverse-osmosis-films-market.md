<p><p>The reverse osmosis films market comprises three main types: cellulose-based, thin film composite, and others. Cellulose-based membranes are derived from natural sources, offering good permeability but lower chemical resistance. Thin film composite membranes, made from synthetic polymers, provide superior performance and durability, making them the preferred choice for most applications. The "others" category includes emerging technologies like ceramic membranes and hybrid materials, which aim to enhance filtration efficiency and reduce fouling in various water treatment processes.</p></p>
