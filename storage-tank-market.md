<p><p>The storage tank market is primarily divided into two main types: aboveground storage tanks (ASTs) and underground storage tanks (USTs). ASTs are used for storing liquids such as fuel, chemicals, and water above the surface, offering easy access and maintenance. USTs, on the other hand, are buried underground, providing protection from environmental elements and space management. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including oil and gas, chemicals, and agriculture, ensuring safe and efficient storage of liquids.</p></p>
