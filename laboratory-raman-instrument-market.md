<p><p>The Laboratory Raman Instrument market is primarily divided into two types: Bench Top Type and Portable Type. In contrast, Portable instruments are compact, lightweight, and designed for field use, enabling on-site analysis with convenience. Both types serve distinct applications, with bench tops favored in controlled environments and portable units ideal for real-time monitoring and analysis in various external settings.</p></p>
