<p><strong>The Laboratory Hydrogen Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Membrane Technology</li><li>PSA Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory hydrogen generators market primarily includes two key technologies: membrane technology and pressure swing adsorption (PSA) technology. Membrane technology utilizes selective permeable membranes to separate hydrogen from other gases, ensuring high purity and efficiency. In contrast, PSA technology relies on adsorbent materials that selectively capture impurities under pressure, subsequently releasing pure hydrogen during depressurization. Both technologies offer reliable, on-demand hydrogen generation suitable for various laboratory applications, emphasizing safety and minimal environmental impact.</p></p>
