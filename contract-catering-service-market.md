<p><strong>The Contract Catering Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Price Contract</li><li>Cost Plus Contract</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contract catering services primarily operate under two market types: Fixed Price Contracts and Cost Plus Contracts. </p><p>In Fixed Price Contracts, a set amount is agreed upon for catering services, offering budget certainty to clients while incentivizing efficiency for the caterer. </p><p>Conversely, Cost Plus Contracts allow caterers to charge clients for actual costs incurred plus a predetermined margin. This type often suits bespoke services, providing flexibility and adaptability to client needs but may lead to higher final costs.</p></p>
