<p><ul><li>Double Coated Type</li><li>Single Coated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer dicing tape market is primarily segmented into two main types: double coated and single coated. Double coated tape features adhesive on both sides, providing strong bonding for securing the wafer during cutting processes while allowing for easy removal post-dicing. Single coated tape, on the other hand, has adhesive on one side only, often used for temporary attachment or specific applications where one-sided adhesion suffices. Both types are crucial in ensuring precision and efficiency in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
