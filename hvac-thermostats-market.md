<p><p>The HVAC thermostats market includes several types tailored for different applications. Plug-in type thermostats are easily installed and ideal for portable systems, providing user-friendly temperature control. Immersion type thermostats are designed to sense temperature changes in liquids, commonly used in water heaters and boilers. Surface type thermostats detect ambient temperatures in enclosed spaces, making them suitable for residential and commercial heating and cooling systems. Each type offers distinct advantages in efficiency, accuracy, and installation flexibility.</p></p>
