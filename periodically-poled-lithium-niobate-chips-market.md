<p><p>Periodically Poled Lithium Niobate (PPLN) chips are essential in nonlinear optical applications. The market can be categorized into three types: Single Grating chips, which feature a uniform poling structure for specific wavelength conversion; Multi-grating chips, designed with varied poling periods to target multiple wavelengths or enhance efficiency; and Fanout Grating chips, characterized by the ability to manage multiple signal inputs/outputs over larger area interactions. Each type serves unique applications in telecommunications, signal processing, and sensing technologies.</p></p>
