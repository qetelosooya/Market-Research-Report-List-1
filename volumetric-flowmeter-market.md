<p><p>The volumetric flowmeter market includes various types designed for precise fluid measurement. Oval gear flow meters utilize rotating gears to measure flow rate. Scraper flowmeters use a scraping mechanism to detect flow changes. Dual rotameters feature two gauges for enhanced accuracy. Rotary piston flow meters measure flow with a rotating piston, while reciprocating piston flow meters rely on reciprocating motion. Disc flowmeters employ a disc to gauge flow, while liquid-sealed drum type gas flow meters and wet gas meters manage gas measurement in varying conditions.</p></p>
