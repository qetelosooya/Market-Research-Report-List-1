<p><strong>The Fume Cupboard Worktop Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ductless Fume Hoods</li><li>Ducted Fume Hoods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fume cupboard worktop market comprises two main types: ductless fume hoods and ducted fume hoods. Ductless fume hoods recirculate air through built-in filters, providing a portable solution ideal for labs without dedicated ventilation systems. In contrast, ducted fume hoods expel hazardous fumes outside, ensuring a safer working environment in areas with proper ventilation infrastructure. Both types serve essential roles in protecting laboratory personnel from toxic substances, but their applications vary based on specific lab requirements and layout.</p></p>
