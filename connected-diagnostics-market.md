<p><p>The Connected Diagnostics market is categorized into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based solutions. On-Premise systems are installed locally within healthcare facilities, offering enhanced control over data security and performance but requiring significant infrastructure investment and maintenance. This flexibility facilitates real-time data sharing and collaboration among healthcare providers and improves patient outcomes.</p></p>
