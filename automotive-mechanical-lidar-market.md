<p><p>The automotive mechanical LiDAR market is primarily categorized into two types: fixed and spiral LiDAR systems. Fixed LiDAR units are stationary sensors that provide a specific field of view, ideal for mapping predefined areas. In contrast, spiral LiDAR systems feature rotating sensors that capture data in a 360-degree pattern, offering comprehensive coverage and enhancing object detection capabilities. Both types play crucial roles in autonomous vehicle navigation, enhancing safety and precision in various driving conditions.</p></p>
