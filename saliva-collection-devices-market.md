<p><p>The saliva collection devices market is categorized into two main types: whole saliva collection devices and glandular saliva collection devices. Whole saliva collection devices capture saliva from the mouth, providing a comprehensive sample that reflects oral and systemic health. In contrast, glandular saliva collection devices specifically target saliva from individual glands, such as the submandibular or parotid glands, allowing for focused analysis of gland-specific conditions and facilitating detailed diagnostics. Both types serve crucial roles in medical diagnostics and research.</p></p>
