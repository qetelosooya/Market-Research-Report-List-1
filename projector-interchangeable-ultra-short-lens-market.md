<p><p>The Projector Interchangeable Ultra Short Lens market includes various types tailored for different projection needs. Straight lenses provide direct imaging, while L(90°) lenses enable vertical projection adjustments. Folding lenses offer versatility; those not at 90° or 180° facilitate angled projections, making them ideal for confined spaces or creative setups. In contrast, 180° folding lenses extend projection options, allowing for ceiling or wall-mounted setups without sacrificing image quality. Each type caters to unique installation challenges and viewer experiences.</p></p>
