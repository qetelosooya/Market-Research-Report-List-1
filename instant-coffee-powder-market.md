<p><strong>The Instant Coffee Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Dry Coffee</li><li>Freeze Dry Coffee</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The instant coffee powder market primarily includes two types: spray-dry coffee and freeze-dry coffee. Spray-dry coffee is produced by rapidly evaporating water from brewed coffee, resulting in fine granules, which preserve flavor and aroma. This method is cost-effective and widely used for mass production. In contrast, freeze-dry coffee involves freezing brewed coffee and then removing moisture under vacuum, which retains more of the original flavor and aroma compared to spray drying, leading to a higher-quality product.</p></p>
