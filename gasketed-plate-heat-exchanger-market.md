<p><p>A gasketed plate heat exchanger is a type of heat exchanger that uses a series of plates, typically made of stainless steel, to transfer heat between two fluids. The plates are held together by gaskets that create a sealed environment, allowing for efficient heat exchange while minimizing the risk of leakage. This design enables a compact and lightweight unit, making gasketed plate heat exchangers ideal for various industrial applications, including food and beverage, chemical processing, and HVAC systems.</p><p>The gasketed plate heat exchanger market is anticipated to witness significant growth, with projections indicating a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.8% during the forecast period.
