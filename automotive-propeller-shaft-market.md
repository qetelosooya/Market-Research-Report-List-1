<p><ul><li>Single Propeller Shaft</li><li>Multi Piece Propeller Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive propeller shaft market comprises two primary types: single propeller shaft and multi-piece propeller shaft. A single propeller shaft is a straightforward, one-piece component that efficiently transfers torque from the transmission to the differential in rear or all-wheel drive vehicles. In contrast, a multi-piece propeller shaft consists of two or more segments linked by universal joints, allowing for greater flexibility and reduced weight, making it suitable for larger vehicles or those with complex drivetrains requiring enhanced articulation.</p></p>
