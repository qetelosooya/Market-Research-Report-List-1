<p><strong>The HVAC Control Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Balancing Valve</li><li>Dynamic Balancing Valve</li><li>PICV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVAC control valve market encompasses various types, each serving unique functions in managing airflow and water flow within heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. Static balancing valves maintain a consistent flow by manually setting resistance, ensuring optimal system performance. Dynamic balancing valves automatically adjust flow rates based on system demands. Pressure Independent Control Valves (PICVs) combine features of both static and dynamic valves, providing accurate flow control regardless of pressure variations, enhancing energy efficiency and system reliability.</p></p>
