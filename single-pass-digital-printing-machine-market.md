<p><p>The single-pass digital printing machine market primarily consists of inkjet and dye-sublimation printing technologies. Inkjet digital printing machines utilize high-speed print heads to deposit ink directly onto substrates, offering versatility and high-quality outputs for various applications. In contrast, dye-sublimation digital printing machines employ heat transfer to infuse dye into materials, ideal for producing vibrant, durable prints on textiles and other surfaces. Both technologies cater to diverse industries, enhancing efficiency and reducing turnaround times in print production.</p></p>
