<p><p>The Double Beam UV-VIS-NIR Spectrophotometer market is segmented into Single Monochromator and Double Monochromator types. Single Monochromator systems use one monochromator for both sample and reference light paths, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for routine analysis. Double Monochromator systems utilize two monochromators, enhancing spectral resolution and sensitivity by minimizing stray light and improving data accuracy. These systems are favored in advanced research applications where precise measurements are crucial, despite their higher cost and complexity.</p></p>
