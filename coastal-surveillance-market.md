<p><p>The coastal surveillance market is segmented into three main types: X-Band Radar, S-Band Radar, and Others. X-Band Radar is suitable for high-resolution target detection, often used for smaller vessels and coastal applications due to its shorter wavelength. S-Band Radar, with its longer wavelength, excels in long-range detection, making it ideal for tracking larger ships and weather monitoring. Meanwhile, the "Others" category encompasses various technologies like AIS and electro-optical sensors that enhance surveillance capabilities through diverse detection methods.</p></p>
