<p><ul><li>Inbound or Receiving Systems</li><li>Storage System</li><li>Disensing or Deliver System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airport fuel hydrant systems consist of three main types: Inbound or Receiving Systems, Storage Systems, and Dispensing or Delivery Systems. Inbound systems manage the intake of fuel from tankers to the airport infrastructure. Storage systems provide safe and regulated containment for the fuel until needed. Finally, dispensing systems facilitate the transfer of fuel from storage to aircraft, ensuring efficient and timely refueling operations. Together, these components create a seamless and reliable fuel supply chain at airports.</p></p>
