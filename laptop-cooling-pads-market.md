<p><p>The laptop cooling pads market features two primary types: active and passive cooling pads. Active cooling pads incorporate built-in fans to enhance airflow, effectively dissipating heat from laptops during intensive tasks. These pads are ideal for users who frequently engage in gaming or resource-heavy applications. Conversely, passive cooling pads rely on materials and design to improve heat dissipation without mechanical components. They are lightweight and portable, making them suitable for general use and travel, albeit with a lower cooling efficiency compared to active options.</p></p>
