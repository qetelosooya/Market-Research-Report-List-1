<p><p>The LIMS market comprises three main types: On-premise LIMS, which are installed locally on a company's servers, offering full control and customization but requiring significant IT resources. Cloud-based LIMS are hosted on remote servers, providing accessibility and scalability with lower upfront costs, ideal for organizations seeking flexibility. Remotely Hosted LIMS combine elements of both, where the software is managed by a third-party provider but accessed via the cloud. Each type offers distinct benefits depending on organizational needs and infrastructure.</p></p>
