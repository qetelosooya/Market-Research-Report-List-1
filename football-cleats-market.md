<p><strong>The Football Cleats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SG</li><li>AG</li><li>FG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Football cleats are categorized into three main types based on the playing surface: SG (Soft Ground), AG (Artificial Ground), and FG (Firm Ground). SG cleats feature longer, removable studs designed for wet, muddy fields, providing optimal traction. AG cleats have shorter, conical studs tailored for artificial turf, ensuring stability without damaging the surface. FG cleats, versatile for natural grass, have a combination of bladed and conical studs for balance, making them ideal for firm, dry pitches. Each type addresses specific field conditions to enhance player performance.</p></p>
