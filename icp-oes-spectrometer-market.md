<p><strong>The ICP-OES Spectrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sequential Type</li><li>Simultaneous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ICP-OES spectrometer market is primarily segmented into two types: sequential and simultaneous. Sequential ICP-OES operates by analyzing one wavelength at a time, providing high sensitivity and resolution, making it suitable for precise elemental analysis. In contrast, simultaneous ICP-OES measures multiple wavelengths simultaneously, allowing for faster throughput and efficiency during analysis, making it ideal for high-volume testing environments. Both types have distinct advantages, catering to different analytical needs and preferences within various industries.</p></p>
