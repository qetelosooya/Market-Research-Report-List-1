<p><ul><li>BWC</li><li>POU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drinking water machine market comprises two main types: Bottleless Water Coolers (BWC) and Point of Use (POU) systems. BWC units provide filtered water directly from a plumbing source without the need for bottled water, promoting sustainability and convenience. POU systems, on the other hand, are installed at specific water access points, such as kitchens or break rooms, delivering purified water instantly and efficiently. Both types cater to residential and commercial needs, enhancing water access and quality.</p></p>
