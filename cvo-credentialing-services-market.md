<p><p>CVO Credentialing Services encompass three primary market types: Initial and Re-credentialing, Expirables Management, and Sanctions Monitoring. Initial and Re-credentialing involves the verification of healthcare professionals' qualifications and experience at the onset and during their careers. Expirables Management focuses on tracking and managing the expiration of licenses, certifications, and other necessary credentials. Sanctions Monitoring ensures ongoing compliance by continuously verifying that professionals are not under any disciplinary actions or sanctions, thereby maintaining quality and safety in healthcare delivery.</p></p>
