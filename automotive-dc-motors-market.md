<p><strong>The Automotive DC Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brushed</li><li>BLDC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive DC motors market is primarily categorized into brushed and brushless DC motors (BLDC). Brushed DC motors are simpler in design, cost-effective, and commonly used for applications like power windows and seat adjustments. However, they require maintenance due to brush wear. In contrast, BLDC motors are more efficient, have better durability, and generate less noise, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as electric vehicles and advanced driver-assistance systems. Both types are integral to modern automotive technology.</p></p>
