<p><ul><li> Rare Earth Intensifying Screen</li><li>Calcium Tungstate Intensifying Screen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical intensifying screen market comprises two main types: rare earth intensifying screens and calcium tungstate intensifying screens. Rare earth screens utilize materials like lanthanum and terbium to enhance image quality by converting radiation into visible light more efficiently, resulting in reduced exposure times and improved detail. In contrast, calcium tungstate screens, composed of calcium tungstate crystals, have been traditionally used in radiography but are less efficient than rare earth options. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing radiographic imaging.</p></p>
