<p><p>The axially split multistage pump market includes single suction and double suction pump types. Single suction pumps draw fluid from one side of the impeller, making them simpler and suitable for applications with lower flow rates. In contrast, double suction pumps have a dual inlet, allowing fluid to enter from both sides, enhancing efficiency and handling higher flow volumes. Both designs are used in various industries, including water treatment and industrial processes, offering distinct advantages based on specific operational needs.</p></p>
