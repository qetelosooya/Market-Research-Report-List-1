<p><p>The Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB) resins market is segmented into higher, medium, lower, and modified molecular weight grades. Higher molecular weight PVB resins offer enhanced mechanical strength and are ideal for applications requiring durability, such as laminated safety glass. Medium molecular weight PVB resins balance flexibility and strength, making them suitable for various coatings and adhesives. Lower molecular weight resins are typically used for specialized applications like inks and paints. Modified PVB resins incorporate additives for improved performance in specific environments, enhancing versatility.</p></p>
