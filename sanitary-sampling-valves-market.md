<p><p>The sanitary sampling valves market is categorized into three main types: threaded connection, welded connection, and flanged connection. Threaded connection valves are easy to install and maintain, making them suitable for low-pressure applications. Welded connection valves offer a more hygienic solution, eliminating potential leakage points, and are ideal for high-purity environments. Flanged connection valves provide robust and secure fittings, facilitating easy assembly and disassembly in larger systems. Each type serves specific industrial requirements in food, beverage, pharmaceutical, and biotechnology sectors.</p></p>
