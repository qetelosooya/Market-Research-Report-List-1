<p><ul><li>Inverted Type</li><li>Upright Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The educational monocular microscopes market comprises two main types: inverted and upright microscopes. Inverted microscopes feature a design that places the light source and optics below the stage, making them ideal for observing live samples in culture dishes. Conversely, upright microscopes position the optics above the stage, suitable for examining solid specimens on slides. Both types cater to educational needs in classrooms and laboratories, offering unique advantages for students to explore biological and materials science effectively.</p></p>
