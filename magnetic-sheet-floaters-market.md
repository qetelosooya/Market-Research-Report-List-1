<p><p>The Magnetic Sheet Floaters market comprises two main types: Permanent Magnet and Electromagnetic systems. Permanent Magnet floaters utilize fixed magnets to create a stable magnetic field, providing consistent buoyancy and support in various industrial applications. In contrast, Electromagnetic floaters employ electric currents to generate adjustable magnetic fields, allowing for dynamic control of buoyancy based on operational needs. Both types serve essential roles in handling and transporting metallic sheets, enhancing efficiency in manufacturing and logistics processes.</p></p>
